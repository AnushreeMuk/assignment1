Vincenzo Cassano

Italian Lawyer & Mafia Consigliere


Tramontate, stelle. All'alba vincero, Vincero, Vincero

[Set, stars. At dawn, I will win, I will win]

Anti-Hero He uses his ruthless Mafia strategies in dealing with his enemies for the good intentions.
The Chessmaster He manipulates events to get the desired outcome.
Sharp-Dressed Man Always seen dressed in expensive, well-tailored suits.
Cold-Blooded Torture He tortures them painfully, both physically and mentally.
Even Evil Has Standards He doesn't kill women and children.
Pay Evil unto Evil Most of the time, he would kill the bad guys the same way they killed their victims.

Good Is Not Soft But in the series a much softer side of Vincenzo had been exposed, only for him to return to the cruel mafia methods in the finale.
